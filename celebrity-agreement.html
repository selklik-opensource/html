(a)        if the Celebrity commits any or repeated breach or non-observance of any of the provisions of this agreement;

(b)        if the Celebrity is incapacitated (including by reason of illness or accident) from providing the Services; or

(c)        if the Celebrity is declared bankrupt or makes any arrangement with or for the benefit of its creditors.

8.2             Without prejudice to the rights and remedies of the Company available under the law, the Company may terminate this agreement immediately upon giving written notice if the Celebrity takes or engages in any action or conduct that brings the Celebrity or the Company into disrepute.

8.3             Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, the Company shall be entitled to discontinue this Agreement without reason and the parties may terminate this agreement by mutual written agreement.

8.4             Upon termination:

(a)              the provisions of clauses 3.3 (e), 3.3 (f), 6 (Mutual Indemnity), 7 (Confidentiality) and clause 10 (No Partnership or Employment), 11 (Notices) and 13 (Governing Law) shall continue in force in accordance with their respective terms;

(c)        the Company will be entitled to continue to use the name, autograph and likeness of the Celebrity and the provisions of clause 4 (Sales Proceeds) will continue to apply for the period commencing upon termination and ending on the date on which the Company ceases to use the name, autograph or likeness of the Celebrity.
